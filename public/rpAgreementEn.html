InWeCrypt Red Packet Use Agreement

1.Notification

　　The InWeCrypto Red Packet Use Agreement (hereinafter referred to as the “Agreement”) is an effective contract between the InWeCrypto Platform (hereinafter referred to as the“Platform”) and your adoption of“Red Packet Creation and Distribution”. When you create a Red Packet, it means you have already reached the agreement with the company and accepted all terms of this agreement. Before accepting this agreement, please read the content of this agreement carefully. If you do not agree to accept any term of this Agreement, or do not understand the meaning of the relevant terms accurately, please do not proceed with the next step.

2.Definition

　　InWe Red Packet: InWe Red Packet provided by InWeCrypto is a marketing tool for targeted funds distribution. Users can deposit their ERC20 token into the wallet and then create an InWe Red Packet and start distribution. The Red Packet title and blessing phrases can be customized by users.

3.Services and Norms

3.1 Services
3.1.1 Red Packet Creation
　　On the platform, Users can create Red Packets and distribute them to participants though activities.
3.1.2 Service Fees
　　The platform charges a minimum of 0.0002 eth for technical service fee, which is determined by the number of distributed Red Packet and the current ETH price, thus is subjected to change with the fluctuation.
3.1.3 Limitations
　　InWe Wallet Distribution Limitations：
　　（1）The number of Red Packets distributed per unit time is limited；
　　（2）The Red Packet distribution is limited by the speed of block confirmations；
　　（3）The Red Packet distribution is limited by the traffic of receivers；
　　Any of the situation above occurs, there will be a delay of Red Packet distribution on the platform.
3.1.4 Red Packet Pre-authorization/Packing, Lottery and Receiving
　　（1）Users need to deposit enough ETH and ERC20 tokens in the InWeCrypto wallet before pre-authorization/packing of the Red Packet.
　　（2）Drawing Red Packet Lottery: if all the Red Packets are opened within 24 hours, the lottery will be drawn once the last packet is opened (which means funds will not be drawn as soon as the packet is opened). If all the Red Packets are not opened within 24 hours, the lottery will be drawn by the end of 24h since its launch. Unopened Red Packets will be returned to the creators’ wallet address.
　　（3）Fund release is determined by the confirmation speed.
3.1.5 Fund Settlement
　　（1）The created Red Packet can be launched through activities, but cannot be revoked.
　　（2）The Red Packet that are being created or have been launched cannot be cancelled. When the activity ends, the unopened Red Packet will be automatically refunded. The service fee and mining gas will not be refunded.
　　（3）In the following cases, the system will refund the Red Packet after the settlement date, along with an indication of the reasons:
　　　　      i. Participants exceeded the time limit set for opening (24 hours) while the Red Packet activities were announced through InWeCrypto Red Packet services.
 　　　　     ii. The InWeCrypto interface received an invalid ETH wallet address thus it is unable to accept the Red Packet.
　　（4）Unless the platform errors result in abnormal services, the service fees will not be refunded.
3.2 Norms
3.2.1 When using the Red Packet on the Platform, the uploaded pictures, names, etc., should be true and legally effective, and will not infringe upon the legal rights and interests of other third parties, including but not limited to reputation rights, portrait rights, intellectual property rights, business secrets and personal privacy.
3.2.2 Blessing phrases customized through the Platform should not violate any rules, regulations, ordinances and laws, as well as relevant industrial regulations. It should not involve any content that violates national laws, endanger national security and unity, social stability, public order, and social customs, or that with pornographic, immoral, fraudulent, defamatory (plus commercially), malicious competition, illegal intimidation, or unlawful harassment.
3.2.3 If the blessing phrases users publish on the platform include marketing advertising content etc., users need to promise:
　　（1）The content is true and legally effective. It should not infringe upon the legitimate rights and interests of any third parties and should not subject to any malicious competition or exaggerated propaganda.
